Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 26, 2026

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

DeenCompanion ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Islamic companion app.

We believe your spiritual journey is deeply personal. That's why we've designed DeenCompanion to respect your privacy by keeping your data on your device.

Information We Collect

DeenCompanion is designed with privacy at its core. Here's what information is used:

Location Data (Optional)
  • Purpose: To calculate accurate prayer times for your location
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device only
  • Sharing: Never shared with third parties
  • Control: You can deny location access; prayer times can be set manually

Local App Data:

  • Prayer tracking and streaks
  • Quran bookmarks and reading progress
  • App preferences and settings
  • Widget display preferences

All of this data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

How We Use Your Information

The information stored on your device is used solely to:

  • Calculate accurate prayer times based on your location
  • Send prayer time notifications (if enabled)
  • Track your prayer streak and progress
  • Remember your Quran reading position and bookmarks
  • Display prayer times in the home screen widget
  • Customize your app experience based on your preferences

We do not use your information for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose beyond app functionality.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share your data. Period.

DeenCompanion does not:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Use third-party analytics services
  • Transmit your data to external servers
  • Track your behavior across apps or websites

Your information stays on your device, under your control.

Data Security

Your data security is important to us:

  • All data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage
  • We use Apple's standard security frameworks
  • No data is transmitted over the internet (except optional location services from Apple)
  • Your data is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)

If you delete the app, all your data is permanently removed from your device.

App Permissions

DeenCompanion may request the following permissions:

Location Services (Optional)

  • Why: To calculate accurate prayer times for your area
  • When: Only when you open the app or when calculating prayer times
  • Control: Can be disabled in iOS Settings; app still works with manual location

Notifications (Optional)

  • Why: To remind you of prayer times
  • When: At scheduled prayer times you've enabled
  • Control: Can be managed in iOS Settings

Both permissions are optional. You can use core app features without granting them.

Children's Privacy

DeenCompanion is designed for users of all ages, including children learning about Islamic practices.

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults. All data remains on the user's device and is not transmitted to us.

Parents: We encourage you to review this policy with your children and help them understand how the app works.

Premium Features and Purchases

If you purchase Premium features:

  • All transactions are processed securely through Apple's App Store
  • We do not collect or store your payment information
  • Apple's privacy policy applies to purchase data
  • Premium status is stored locally on your device
  • You can restore purchases across your devices using your Apple ID

Data Retention and Deletion

Your Data, Your Control

  • All data is stored on your device only
  • You can delete the app at any time to remove all data
  • Deleting the app permanently removes all your prayer tracking, bookmarks, and preferences
  • We cannot recover your data because we never receive it

If you want to keep your data when getting a new device, use iCloud backup before transferring.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in:

  • App features and functionality
  • Legal requirements
  • Privacy best practices

When we make changes:

  • The "Last Updated" date will be revised
  • Significant changes will be communicated through an in-app notification
  • Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

Privacy as an Islamic Value

"Do not spy on one another" (Quran 49:12)

We believe protecting your privacy is not just good practice—it's a reflection of Islamic principles of trust, respect, and dignity.
